Free Shop

2011, - LONDON, UK

[+] click to read more

Operating under 3 simple rules:

  • Take whatever you want
  • Dump only useful stuff
  • No refunds

The Free shop was created as a simple way to acquire things you like, or get rid of stuff you don’t want anymore. Based on the experience of different squats around Spain and Germany, the idea of having a store where you don’t have to pay challenges the principles of market economy and gives trading a new meaning. For a long time Free shop served people in the Arena warehouses. To get rid or adquire loads of different things such as caps, shoes, clothing, perfumes, books, umbrellas, tents, etc. Unfortunalety was brutally attack and destroyed by a horrible guy called Terry.

That Winning Feeling

2011 - LONDON, UK

[+] click to read more

That Winning feeling was a promotional campaign for Gamesys and its website Jackpotjoy.com, integrating social media into a gambling website. The costumer retention mechanic allows people to simply upload a picture from their computers or webcam and type 80 characters to say what would they do if they’d win the prize. Every Friday the top five photos won a £1,000 cash prize each.

During October 2011 “That Winning Feeling” promotion was tied with a TV campaign appearing to be extremely popular, resulting in a total of 5,196 entries from 3,775 unique members and getting for Gamesys the first place in the EGR:E-Gaming Review Marketing Campaign of the Year. Later on the promotion was redesigned for the Spanish website and also a Christmas version called “That Festive Feeling”, was released on December 2011, adding a voting functionality.
